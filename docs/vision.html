The vision for the Cultural Economics project (Cult/Econ)

For now, this is a small side project, where I've spent a few hours gather some of my favourite learning resources, and others I've come across to cover a few bases of what I'm calling traditional skills; something we as a society are fundamentally lacking.

Inspired by Permaculture, Doomer Optimism, Solarpunk, Retrosuburbia, this is a community resource built to support the learning and accessibility of important skills and crafts..

In the coming months, I will open-source the code, but for the time being, you can submit any useful resources or requests through this handy form.

Topics I'd like to eventually cover:

  1. Blacksmithing:
    • Basic forging techniques
    • Tool making
    • Knife making
    • Metal sculpture
  2. Canning and Food Preservation:
    • Water bath canning
    • Pressure canning
    • Fermentation
    • Drying and dehydrating
    • Smoking and curing meats
  3. Natural Medicine and Herbalism:
    • Plant identification
    • Medicinal herb cultivation
    • Herbal remedies and tinctures
    • Wildcrafting and foraging
  4. Handwriting and Calligraphy:
    • Cursive writing
    • Hand lettering
    • Calligraphy styles (e.g., italic, gothic, brush
      • lettering)
  5. Shoemaking and Leathercraft:
    • Pattern making
    • Leather cutting and stitching
    • Shoe construction
    • Leather tooling and carving
  6. Traditional Carpentry and Woodworking:
    • Joinery techniques (e.g., dovetail, mortise and tenon)
    • Woodturning
    • Furniture making
    • Wooden boat building
  7. Spinning and Weaving:
    • Spindle spinning
    • Wheel spinning
    • Loom weaving
    • Fiber preparation
  8. Tinsmithing and Metalwork:
    • Tin punching
    • Metal embossing
    • Repoussé (metal shaping from the reverse side)
    • Metal casting
  9. Basic Sewing and Mending:
    • Hand sewing techniques
    • Machine sewing
    • Patching and darning
    • Garment alterations
  10. Navigation and Orienteering:
    • Map reading
    • Compass use
    • Land navigation
    • Celestial navigation
  11. Traditional Cooking and Food Preparation:
    • Food preservation (canning, pickling)
    • Bread making
    • Fermentation (sauerkraut, kimchi)
    • Cooking with open flame (campfire cooking)
  12. Pottery and Ceramics:
    • Handbuilding techniques (pinch, coil, slab)
    • Wheel throwing
    • Glazing and firing
    • Kiln operation
  13. Beekeeping:
    • Hive management
    • Honey extraction
    • Beeswax processing
    • Pollinator-friendly gardening
  14. Fishing and Angling:
    • Knot tying
    • Bait making
    • Fly tying
    • Fish cleaning and preparation
  15. Rope Making and Knotting:
    • Rope twisting and braiding
    • Knot tying for various purposes (camping, boating,
      • climbing)
    • Splicing techniques
  16. Traditional Music Instrument Crafting:
    • String instrument making (guitar, violin)
    • Woodwind instrument making (flute, whistle)
    • Drum construction
    • Music notation and composition
  17. Natural Building Techniques:
    • Cob construction
    • Wattle and daub
    • Thatching
    • Timber framing
  18. Hunting and Trapping:
    • Archery
    • Trapping techniques
    • Tracking and stalking
    • Butchering and game preparation
  19. Natural Fiber Crafts:
    • Basket weaving
    • Rope and twine making from natural fibers
    • Macramé
    • Spinning plant fibers (e.g., cotton, flax)
  20. Wood Carving:
    • Relief carving
    • Whittling
    • Chip carving
    • Carving functional items (utensils, bowls)
  21. Metal Engraving:
    • Hand engraving techniques
    • Tool and knife embellishment
    • Metal inlay
    • Repousse and chasing
  22. Quilting and Patchwork:
    • Quilt block construction
    • Appliqué
    • Hand quilting
    • Quilt binding techniques
  23. Traditional Clothing Construction:
    • Sewing garments from scratch
    • Historical costume making
    • Tailoring and pattern drafting
    • Embroidery and embellishment
  24. Broom Making:
    • Broomcorn cultivation and processing
    • Broom design and construction
    • Decorative broom techniques
    • Broom maintenance and care
